About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Hamscom Care emerged from a simple passion: to elevate care standards through empathy and expertise. Today, we partner with over 20 Asian and UK facilities and have trained over 100 caregivers.

Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name in healthcare staffing across the United Kingdom. We believe that quality care begins with quality training and compassionate professionals.
